# MotivAid

## User Stories & Acceptance Criteria

---

## 1. Authentication & User Management

### US-01: User Login

**As a** midwife
**I want** to securely log into the MotivAid app
**So that** I can access clinical and training features relevant to my role.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* User can log in using approved credentials.
* Invalid credentials return a clear error message.
* User role (midwife, supervisor, admin) is correctly assigned after login.

---

### US-02: Role-Based Access Control

**As a** system administrator
**I want** users to only see features permitted to their role
**So that** data access and workflows remain secure.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* Midwives cannot access admin-only screens.
* Supervisors can view aggregated reports but not modify system settings.
* Admins have full system access.

---

## 2. Maternal Risk Assessment

### US-03: Capture Maternal Risk Factors

**As a** midwife
**I want** to enter maternal risk factors before delivery
**So that** the app can assess PPH risk early.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* Risk factor form includes parity, anemia, PPH history, and multiple pregnancy.
* Required fields are validated before submission.
* Data is saved locally if offline.

---

### US-04: Generate Risk Profile

**As a** midwife
**I want** the app to flag high-risk mothers
**So that** I can prepare for potential PPH.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* App calculates risk level automatically.
* High-risk cases are clearly highlighted.
* Preparedness recommendations are displayed.

---

## 3. Clinical Mode (E-MOTIVE Workflow)

### US-05: Activate Clinical Mode

**As a** midwife
**I want** to activate clinical mode immediately after delivery
**So that** I receive real-time PPH guidance.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* Clinical mode can be activated with one action.
* Timer starts automatically upon activation.
* The E-MOTIVE checklist is displayed.

---

### US-06: Step-by-Step E-MOTIVE Guidance

**As a** midwife
**I want** structured step-by-step prompts
**So that** I correctly apply the E-MOTIVE bundle.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* Steps follow the E-MOTIVE sequence.
* User cannot skip critical steps without acknowledgment.
* Completed steps are visually marked.

---

### US-07: Shock Index Calculation

**As a** midwife
**I want** the app to calculate shock index automatically
**So that** I can identify maternal deterioration early.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* Shock index is calculated using HR and SBP.
* Unsafe values trigger alerts.
* Calculation works offline.

---

## 4. Alerts & Escalation

### US-08: Clinical Alerts

**As a** midwife
**I want** to receive alerts when bleeding thresholds are exceeded
**So that** I can escalate care promptly.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* Alerts trigger at >500ml and >1000ml blood loss.
* Alerts are visually and audibly distinct.
* Alerts display recommended actions.

---

### US-09: Emergency Escalation

**As a** midwife
**I want** to notify the emergency team with one tap
**So that** help is mobilized quickly.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* Emergency button is always accessible in clinical mode.
* Notifications are sent via SMS or in-app messaging.
* Action is logged with timestamp.

---

## 5. Documentation & Audit

### US-10: Automatic Case Documentation

**As a** midwife
**I want** the app to automatically record all interventions
**So that** I do not lose critical data during emergencies.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* All actions are timestamped automatically.
* Data is stored locally when offline.
* No manual duplication is required.

---

### US-11: Generate PPH Case Report

**As a** supervisor
**I want** to view standardized PPH case reports
**So that** I can conduct audits and reviews.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* Reports include interventions, timelines, and outcomes.
* Reports are read-only for supervisors.
* Data access is facility-restricted.

---

## 6. Training & Simulation Mode

### US-12: Access Training Mode

**As a** midwife
**I want** to practice PPH scenarios
**So that** I improve my clinical confidence.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* Training mode is accessible outside clinical mode.
* Scenarios simulate real PPH cases.
* No clinical data is affected.

---

### US-13: Quizzes & Assessments

**As a** midwife
**I want** to take quizzes after training
**So that** I can assess my understanding.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* Quizzes are auto-scored.
* Immediate feedback is provided.
* Results are stored for performance tracking.

---

## 7. Offline & Sync

### US-14: Offline Usage

**As a** midwife
**I want** the app to work without internet
**So that** I can use it in low-connectivity settings.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* All core features work offline.
* User is informed of offline status.
* No data loss occurs.

---

### US-15: Data Synchronization

**As a** system administrator
**I want** offline data to sync automatically
**So that** records remain complete.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* Sync occurs when connectivity is restored.
* Conflicts are resolved safely.
* User is notified of sync status.

---

## 8. Reporting & Analytics

### US-16: View Adherence Metrics

**As a** program manager
**I want** to view E-MOTIVE adherence metrics
**So that** I can evaluate program performance.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* Metrics are aggregated by facility.
* Individual patient data is anonymized.
* Data updates reflect synced records.
